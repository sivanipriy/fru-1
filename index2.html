Fruit Gallery

Apple

Apples are nutritious fruits that come in various colors, primarily red, green, and yellow. They are known for their sweet taste and crisp texture.

Banana

Bananas are elongated, yellow fruits with a sweet taste. They are a good source of potassium and make for a convenient snack.

Cherry

Cherries are small, round fruits that are typically red or black. They have a sweet or tart flavor and are often used in desserts.

Date

Dates are sweet fruits that grow on date palm trees. They are chewy and often used in Middle Eastern cuisine and desserts.

Elderberry

Elderberries are small, dark berries known for their medicinal properties. They are often used in syrups and jams.
